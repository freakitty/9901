EVASION


Djo'R is a young rapper from the Paris suburbs (95), who decided to name his musical universe "Evasion" enabling him to forget the reality that surrounds him. Eponymous name of his next EP. Once his notoriety installed in the music industry, He will try to develop his universe in the textile. Several universes transcribed in different forms, "L'Evasion" represents as much the exceeding of limits, as the embedding in a system recumbent in a system.

GEEK

GEEK


Professionnal Record. A geek is passionate about one or more specific fields, often related to "imaginar culture" (Cinema, comics, video games, etc.), science, technology and computer science. Because of his sharp knowledge, the geek is sometimes perceived as too cerebral. I decided to illustrate the stereotype of the Geek. First, because the illustration is a medium to put in a form an idea. Secondly, I think it best matches to the topic imagined as such by uninitiated people. As well as those who self-proclaim themselves "Geek" without actually being so and look for the "type" behavior that end up feeding the stereotype. Finally the Geek who "suffers" the consequences of stereotypes, sometimes despite himself. For all that it happens to him to have the feet in the real world as well as its own universe.
Photoshop. 06.19.2016.
By Parys.
